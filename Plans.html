iSwitch Project

Business Plan & Feasibility Study — Digital Moving Services in Slovakia

1. Main Project Goal

Our main goal is to create exceptional moving services for people living in Slovakia. We believe that everything we do should be simple without any stressful matter so that everyone could be satisfied.

2. Secondary Goals

2.1 Technological Goals

  • Within 6 months we want to launch an online website and mobile app that allows customers to get instant quotes, track their moving van in real time and manage bookings digitally.

2.2 Operational Goals

  • Establish a fully functioning moving service with trained staff, modern equipment, and a digital booking system within the first 6 months after launching the online website and mobile app.

2.3 Marketing Goals

  • Within 8 months after official establishment we want to achieve first 1000th customer milestone.
  • Our strategy is to invest in digital marketing especially to collaborate with some influencers, so we could aim more for the young couples that are moving to their new homes.

3. Project Benefits

3.1 For Customers

  • Reliable services with professionals in their field with great insurance coverage.
  • Transparent pricing and real-time tracking of trucks.
  • Hassle-free booking through our easy to use website or app.

3.2 For Society / Environment

  • Using recyclable packaging materials and efficient route planning to reduce carbon footprint.
  • Many job opportunities and experiences in this field.

3.3 For the Company

  • Opportunity to build strong brand and loyal customers through digital convenience.
  • Efficiency through route optimization and digital management are going to enlarge our marging potential.

4. Target Customers

  • Young couples and families relocating from their parents.
  • Young students moving from their home for studies.
  • Small and medium-sized businesses relocating offices or equipment.
  • Customers who value time, convenience and reliability for a great price.

4.1 Demographics

  • Age mostly ranging from 25–55.
  • Urban areas (starting in Košice but later expanding into other “big” cities).
  • All income levels.
  • Everyone using electronic devices.

5. Market & Competition Analysis

5.1 Market Overview

  • In today's market the moving industry is always expanding especially in the big cities where people are constantly switching places mostly due to career changes, rental flexibility, and property market dynamics.
  • Customers expect digital convenience(simple booking, online payments, progress tracking).

5.2 Competitors

  • Local moving companies (Sťahovanie-Express, ProfiMove, stahovat.sk…) – worse prices and smaller moving network.

5.3 iSwitch Competitive Advantage

  • Serious web and app tech approach (instant pricing list without any registration, live truck tracing, simple yet effective design).
  • Transparent pricing: No hidden fees – clear price breakdown per load.
  • Customer care: 24/7 support, insurance, and satisfaction guarantee.
  • Sustainability: Eco-friendly packaging and route optimization to reduce emissions.

6. Financial Viability & Growth Potential

  • Initial funding: We are truly dependent on potential investors who are willing to make this idea possible

6.1 Revenue Streams

  • Moving services
  • Business relocation contracts
  • Premium services (packing/unpacking, storage, cleaning)

Profitability target: Break-even within 20–24 months through repeat customers, partnerships with real estate agencies and people’s reviews.

7. Summary of Project Viability

iSwitch offers a scalable and competitive solution to a common problem - moving homes. The project is viable because it combines: Cheap and reliable moving services, digital convenience (app + tracking), strong focus on customer experience and potential expansion to new cities and many business to business (B2B) partnerships.

Feasibility Study — iSwitch Project

1. Introduction and Project Information

Main goal: To create exceptional, simple, and stress-free moving services for customers living in Slovakia.

Current Status: The moving services market in Slovakia's major cities is growing, but existing providers fail to meet demand for digital convenience (simple online booking, payments, tracking).

Project Origin: Rooted in identifying this gap: need for a technologically advanced, transparent, and reliable moving service.

Main Goal: Establish a leading digital platform for relocation services that minimizes customer stress through transparency and reliability.

2. Market Analysis, Marketing Strategy, and Target Group

Target Group Identification

  • Young couples and families (25–55) in major cities (starting with Košice).
  • Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs) who value time and convenience.

Market Analysis

  • Local competitors often offer worse pricing or have smaller network.
  • iSwitch competitive advantage: technological approach, transparency, sustainability.

Marketing Strategy

  • Investments in digital marketing, collaborations with influencers targeting young couples.

3. Financial Plan and Project Analysis

Revenue Streams

  • Core moving services (residential)
  • Business relocation contracts (B2B)
  • Premium services (packing/unpacking, storage, cleaning)

Economic Evaluation

  • Dependent on initial funding from investors
  • Profitability target: Break-even within 20–24 months through route optimization, digital management, and customer loyalty

4. Project Implementation Plan

  • Technical Implementation: Launch website/app in 6 months, instant quotes and booking management
  • Operational Implementation: Staff hiring, equipment, insurance processes within 6 months
  • Marketing Implementation: Achieve 1,000 customers milestone within 8 months

5. Risk Analysis & Mitigation

  • Technical Risk: Delay/integration issues — mitigation via iterative testing
  • Competition Risk: Aggressive pricing — mitigation via emphasizing non-price advantages
  • Financial Risk: Dependence on initial funding — mitigation via strict CAPEX control and contingency plan

6. Conclusion

The iSwitch project demonstrates high viability, combining technological convenience, transparency, economic soundness, job creation, and sustainable development.
