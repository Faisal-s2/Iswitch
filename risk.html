iSwitch Project Risk Analysis

Risk Description Probability Impact Mitigation Strategy
Insufficient Investment Capital The project relies on investors (70% of funding) – delays or reduced financing may threaten launch. Medium High Diversify funding sources – contact more investors, prepare a backup plan (bank loan, crowdfunding).
Technical Issues with Web and App iSwitch is based on digital platforms – system failures may disrupt operations or reduce customer trust. Medium High Conduct thorough testing before launch, regular updates, and provide 24/7 technical support.
Poor Marketing Response / Low Demand Despite investments in influencers and online ads, the planned number of customers may not be reached. Medium Medium to High Continuously monitor campaign effectiveness, adjust targeting, strengthen B2B partnerships with real estate agencies.
Operational Issues (Logistics, Staff) Lack of trained staff or inefficient moving organization may cause delays or complaints. Medium High Implement staff training, use GPS and route planning, establish internal quality standards.
Competition and Price Pressure Local companies may lower prices or copy iSwitch's digital features. High Medium Maintain competitive advantage – transparent pricing, eco-friendly packaging, high-quality customer support.
Economic Instability / Decreased Demand Economic downturn may reduce willingness to pay for premium services. Low to Medium Medium Offer flexible service packages, focus on cost efficiency, build customer loyalty.
